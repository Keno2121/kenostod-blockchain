## ❓ **FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS**

### **"Do I need to do ALL of this?"**
No! The day-by-day plan above is the minimum. The rest is optional for when you're ready.

### **"What if I don't have time to email people?"**
That's OK! Just post on social media and share your landing page link. That's a perfectly valid launch.

### **"I've never done marketing before - will this work?"**
Yes! Your product is genuinely innovative. Just share it authentically with people who might find it interesting. Don't overthink it.

### **"What if nobody buys tokens on day 1?"**
Totally normal! ICOs take time to build momentum. Some of the biggest ICOs started slow. Focus on reaching more people over the 30-day private sale period.

### **"Do I need to film YouTube videos?"**
No! Videos are optional. The scripts are there if you want to use them later, but launching without videos is 100% fine.

### **"What if someone asks a technical question I can't answer?"**
Be honest: "That's a great question - let me research that and get back to you." Or direct them to your whitepaper/technical docs.
