## 📰 **PRESS RELEASE BASICS**

### **What is a Press Release?**
A formal announcement sent to journalists, bloggers, and media outlets to generate news coverage. Well-written press releases can get you featured in major crypto publications like CoinDesk, CoinTelegraph, Decrypt, and Benzinga.

### **Distribution Channels:**
- **Crypto News Sites:** CoinTelegraph, CoinDesk, Decrypt, Bitcoin.com, NewsBTC
- **Press Release Wires:** PR Newswire, Business Wire, GlobeNewswire, PRWeb
- **Direct Outreach:** Email journalists who cover ICOs and blockchain
- **Your Website:** Publish in a "Press" or "News" section
- **Social Media:** Share with #PressRelease hashtag

### **Best Practices:**
- ✅ Write in third person (avoid "we" - use "Kenostod" or "the company")
- ✅ Include quotes from leadership
- ✅ Keep under 500 words
- ✅ Include contact information
- ✅ Add boilerplate "About" section
- ✅ Use AP Style formatting
- ✅ Include high-resolution images/logos
- ✅ Send on Tuesday-Thursday mornings (best engagement)
