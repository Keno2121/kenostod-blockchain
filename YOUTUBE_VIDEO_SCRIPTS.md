## 📹 **VIDEO PRODUCTION BASICS FOR BEGINNERS**

### **Equipment You Need:**
- 📱 **Smartphone camera** (iPhone/Android - perfectly fine!)
- 🎤 **Microphone** (optional but recommended - $20-50 lavalier mic)
- 💡 **Good lighting** (natural window light or $30 ring light)
- 🖥️ **Screen recording software** (free: OBS Studio, QuickTime, Windows Game Bar)

### **Filming Setup:**
1. **Lighting:** Sit facing a window (natural light) or use a ring light in front of you
2. **Background:** Clean, professional space (bookshelf, plain wall, or branded backdrop)
3. **Camera Position:** Eye level, 2-3 feet away, centered
4. **Audio:** Quiet room, close to mic, test sound first

### **Editing Software (Free Options):**
- **DaVinci Resolve** (professional, free)
- **iMovie** (Mac)
- **Windows Video Editor** (Windows)
- **CapCut** (beginner-friendly)

### **Publishing Checklist:**
- ✅ Upload in 1080p HD
- ✅ Add closed captions/subtitles
- ✅ Use custom thumbnail (Canva.com - free)
- ✅ Include links in description
- ✅ Add hashtags: #KENO #ICO #Cryptocurrency #Blockchain #Education

## 📝 **VIDEO PRODUCTION TIPS**

### **Before Recording:**
1. ✅ Write bullet points (don't memorize word-for-word)
2. ✅ Practice 2-3 times
3. ✅ Test audio levels
4. ✅ Check lighting and framing
5. ✅ Clear background of clutter

### **During Recording:**
1. 🎭 Be yourself - authenticity wins
2. 😊 Smile and show enthusiasm
3. 👀 Look at the camera lens, not the screen
4. 🗣️ Speak clearly and slightly slower than normal
5. ⏸️ Pause between sections (easier to edit)
6. 🔄 Record multiple takes - you can pick the best one

### **Common Beginner Mistakes to Avoid:**
- ❌ Recording in noisy environment
- ❌ Too far from camera (get close!)
- ❌ Poor lighting (dark or harsh shadows)
- ❌ Reading script robotically
- ❌ Rushing through content
- ❌ Forgetting call-to-action

### **Thumbnail Design Tips:**
- Use bright, contrasting colors
- Include your face (close-up with expression)
- Add text (5-7 words max): "KENO ICO - 5X Returns?"
- Use Canva.com templates (free)
- Test on mobile size (50% of viewers)

**Monitor These Metrics:**

- Subscriber conversion

**Optimize Based On:**
