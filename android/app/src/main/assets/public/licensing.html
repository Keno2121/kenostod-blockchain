Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a commercial license?

A commercial license grants you the right to use our proprietary features in your commercial products or services. This includes full access to the technology, technical documentation, API access, implementation support, and ongoing updates.

Can I use these features without a license?

Our features are available for personal, educational, and non-commercial use without a license. However, any commercial use requires a valid commercial license agreement. See our Terms of Service for details.

How long does licensing take?

Once you contact us, we typically schedule an initial consultation within 2-3 business days. License agreements are usually finalized within 1-2 weeks, depending on the complexity of your requirements.

Do you offer custom feature development?

Yes! Enterprise and Custom license tiers include options for custom feature development. We can adapt our technology to meet your specific business needs or develop new features tailored to your use case.

What kind of support do you provide?

All license tiers include technical support. Support levels vary from email support (Startup) to 24/7 dedicated support (Custom). We provide implementation assistance, troubleshooting, and ongoing maintenance updates.

Can I get exclusive rights to a feature?

Exclusive licensing arrangements are available under our Custom License tier. Contact us to discuss exclusivity options for specific features or industry verticals.

Do you offer revenue sharing models?

Yes, we're open to revenue sharing and partnership arrangements, particularly for innovative use cases that expand the reach of our technology. This is typically structured under our Custom License tier.

What happens if I violate the license terms?

License violations may result in immediate suspension of services, legal action, and financial penalties. We take intellectual property protection seriously. However, we work with our partners to ensure compliance and resolve any unintentional violations.
