# FinCEN Form 107 Registration Guide
## Money Services Business (MSB) Registration for Kenostod Blockchain Academy LLC

---

## Overview

FinCEN Form 107 is the federal registration form for Money Services Businesses. This guide walks through completing the registration for Kenostod Blockchain Academy LLC.

**Filing URL:** https://bsaefiling.fincen.treas.gov  
**Cost:** FREE  
**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks for acknowledgment  
**Renewal:** Every 2 years

## After Filing

### What Happens Next

1. **Immediate:** Receive confirmation number
2. **2-4 Weeks:** FinCEN acknowledgment letter
3. **Ongoing:** 
   - Maintain AML program
   - File SARs/CTRs as needed
   - Renew registration every 2 years

### Your MSB Registration Number

After approval, you'll receive an MSB registration number. Use this when:
- Opening bank accounts
- Applying to banking partners (Treasury Prime, etc.)
- Responding to regulatory inquiries

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Wrong activity selected** - Make sure "Money Transmitter" is checked
2. **Missing beneficial owners** - List all 25%+ owners
3. **Incomplete addresses** - Use full street addresses
4. **Wrong EIN** - Double-check the number
5. **Late filing** - Must file within 180 days of starting activities

## State Considerations

### Wyoming (Your Current LLC)
- No state money transmitter license required
- Only federal FinCEN registration needed
- Similar to Montana

### Multi-State Operations
- FinCEN registration covers federal requirements
- Individual states may require separate licenses
- Wyoming/Montana residents = no additional state license
- Serving other states = may trigger their licensing requirements

### States Requiring Separate Licenses
If serving customers in these states, additional licenses may be needed:
- New York (BitLicense)
- California
- Texas
- Florida
- (Most other states)

**Recommendation:** Initially limit service to Wyoming/Montana residents, then expand as you obtain additional state licenses.
