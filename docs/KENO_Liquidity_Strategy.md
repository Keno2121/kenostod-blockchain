## Risk Management & Contingencies

### Market Risk: Price Volatility

**If KENO drops below $0.90:**
- **Trigger:** Activate marketing campaign emphasizing educational value
- **Response:** Partner with B2C2 to support price floor with market-making
- **Timeline:** 2-4 weeks recovery target

**If KENO spikes above $2.00:**
- **Trigger:** Increase course completion incentives (maintain organic growth)
- **Response:** Introduce new KENO sinks (FAL minimum deposits, pool creation fees)
- **Timeline:** Prevent speculative excess within 1-2 weeks

### Liquidity Risk: Low Trading Volume

**If daily volume stays below $100K in Feb 2026:**
- Pre-B2C2 condition: Kenostod provides market-making via internal trading engine
- Post-B2C2: B2C2's guaranteed volume covers this scenario
- Fallback: Increase educational incentives (arbitrage competitions with KENO prizes)

### Regulatory Risk

**US Regulation (FinCEN/SEC):**
- KENO positioned as **educational reward** (not security)
- Compliance: Ensure all US corporate customers are accredited investors or educational institutions
- B2C2 regulatory team consults on classification per jurisdiction

**EU Regulation (MiCA):**
- BEP-20 token qualifies as "token" under MiCA Article 3
- Comply with CAMEL requirements (Capital, Asset management, Market integrity, ESG, Liquidity)
- B2C2 experience with EU regulation critical
