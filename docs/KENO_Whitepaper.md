## Abstract

KENO introduces a revolutionary asset class: the **Knowledge Yield Token (KYT)**. Unlike traditional cryptocurrencies acquired through speculation, KENO is exclusively **earned through education** and **generates passive income** through proprietary Flash Arbitrage Loan (FAL) technology. This creates a self-sustaining ecosystem where knowledge directly converts to wealth.

Kenostod Academy is the educational platform that issues KENO tokens to students who complete its 21-course blockchain curriculum. Graduates enter a perpetual profit-sharing system, creating the first truly democratized wealth-building model in crypto.

**Core Philosophy:** *"KENO is king, Kenostod is his kingdom — but everyone receives royalties, not just the king."*

## 7. Flash Arbitrage Loan Technology

### FAL (Flash Arbitrage Loans)

Proprietary technology enabling:
- **Instant Loans:** Borrow KENO without collateral
- **Same-Block Repayment:** Execute arbitrage within single transaction
- **Risk-Free Trading:** Automatic rollback if unprofitable
- **Educational Access:** Learn real DeFi strategies safely

### FALP (Flash Arbitrage Loan Pools)

Collective liquidity system:
- Users pool KENO tokens
- Platform executes arbitrage on their behalf
- Profits distributed proportionally
- Risk tiers (Conservative, Balanced, Aggressive)
- Lock period bonuses (7-90 days)

### Arbitrage Incentive Protocol (AIP)

- Performance-based rewards
- Reputation system for consistent earners
- Scheduled arbitrage competitions
- Leaderboard rankings
