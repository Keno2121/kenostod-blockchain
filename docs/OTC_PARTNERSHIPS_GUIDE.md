## WHAT OTC PARTNERS WILL ASK FOR

When approaching OTC desks, prepare:

### Documentation
- [ ] Token contract address & verification
- [ ] Tokenomics document
- [ ] Team information (KYC ready)
- [ ] Legal entity documentation
- [ ] Proof of exchange listings
- [ ] Trading volume history

### Business Information
- [ ] Use case for OTC (why large trades?)
- [ ] Expected trade sizes
- [ ] Frequency of trades
- [ ] Custody preferences
- [ ] Settlement preferences (crypto vs fiat)

### Compliance
- [ ] AML policy
- [ ] KYC procedures
- [ ] Source of funds documentation
- [ ] Accredited investor verification (if applicable)

## ALTERNATIVE: Market Makers

Instead of OTC desks, consider **Market Makers** for liquidity:

### Recommended Market Makers
1. **Wintermute** - Also does market making
2. **GSR Markets** - Major crypto market maker
3. **Kairon Labs** - Smaller projects welcome
4. **Empirica** - Helps with MEXC listings + market making

### What Market Makers Do
- Provide buy/sell liquidity on exchanges
- Keep spreads tight
- Prevent large price swings
- Typically charge monthly fee + token loan
