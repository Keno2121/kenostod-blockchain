## 💡 **PRO TIPS**

1. **Personalize Every Email:** Mention something specific about their past investments or focus areas
2. **Follow Up:** 80% of deals come from follow-ups, not initial emails
3. **Be Persistent:** Expect 80-90% no response. That's normal!
4. **Track Everything:** Use the spreadsheet religiously
5. **Stay Organized:** Set calendar reminders for follow-ups
6. **Be Responsive:** Reply to investor emails within 24 hours
