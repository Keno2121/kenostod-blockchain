## 💡 TIPS FOR SOCIAL MEDIA SUCCESS

1. **Use Hashtags Strategically:**
   - Twitter: 3-5 hashtags max
   - Instagram: 15-30 hashtags
   - LinkedIn: 3-5 hashtags

2. **Engage with Comments:**
   - Respond within 1 hour
   - Answer questions thoroughly
   - Thank people for support

3. **Share User-Generated Content:**
   - Repost investors who share your content
   - Feature student testimonials (when you have them)
   - Share media mentions

4. **Be Consistent:**
   - Post daily on at least one platform
   - Keep your messaging consistent
   - Use the same branding across all platforms

5. **Track Performance:**
   - Monitor engagement (likes, shares, comments)
   - See which posts drive traffic to your site
   - Double down on what works
