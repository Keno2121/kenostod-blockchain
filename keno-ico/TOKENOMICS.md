## Deflationary Mechanisms

1. **Token Burning** - 2% of all transaction fees burned
2. **RVT Buyback** - Revenue from RVT royalties used to buy and burn KENO
3. **Merchant Fees** - 0.5% of merchant gateway fees burned
4. **Scheduled Buyback** - Treasury executes quarterly buybacks

**Expected Annual Burn:** 5-10% of circulating supply

- **Uses:** Development funding, partnerships, ecosystem growth
- **Transparency:** All transactions publicly visible on-chain

---

## Security Features

1. **Pausable** - Owner can pause transfers in emergency
2. **Burnable** - Any holder can burn their own tokens
3. **Whitelist** - Initial transfer restrictions to prevent dumping
4. **Reentrancy Protection** - Prevents double-spending attacks
5. **Ownership** - Can be transferred or renounced to DAO
