⚖️ Regulatory Transparency — What We're Solving and When
Banking License
B.U.K. operates as a neobank — not a chartered bank. Deposits held by BaaS partner bank (Treasury Prime, Unit, or Column). B.U.K. is the technology + brand layer.
BaaS Partner Model
Insurance License
Smart contract insurance requires a licensed InsurTech underwriter. B.U.K. is the distribution technology — the licensed insurer carries the regulatory burden per state.
InsurTech Partner Model
FinCEN MSB Registration
Required for any business transmitting money or handling crypto conversions. Pre-seed funds cover FinCEN MSB filing. KYC/AML policies written during compliance phase.
