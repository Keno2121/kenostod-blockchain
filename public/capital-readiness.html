KENO Token Utility Classification Memo
"Is KENO a security?"
No. KENO is a utility token. It is earned through verifiable labor (completing education), not purchased as an investment. It grants access to specific services (device activation, storage, mesh network). It does not represent equity, profit-sharing, or ownership in Kenostod Blockchain LLC.
"How is KENO earned?"
Through completing verified courses in the Academy (21 courses), through Resilience Mining uptime rewards, through mesh relay fees paid by neighbors during outages, and through referral and RVT royalty programs.
"What is KENO's utility?"
Staked to activate Solar Bunker devices. Burned for additional encrypted storage. Spent for priority sync. Paid to neighbors for mesh bandwidth. Staked for Guardian Node status. Governance voting at Diamond tier. Loads onto KUTL Card for real-world spending.
"Is KENO inflationary?"
No. Fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 KENO on BNB Smart Chain. Storage fees are burned (deflationary). Staking locks supply. No mechanism exists to mint new KENO beyond the fixed supply.
"Can KENO be purchased on open market?"
KENO is earned through the Academy and ecosystem participation. Secondary market trading is not the primary distribution mechanism. Primary access is through education and network contribution — not speculation.
