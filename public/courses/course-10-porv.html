⚡ The Problem with Traditional PoW

Bitcoin's Proof-of-Work consumes more electricity than entire countries — all to solve arbitrary mathematical puzzles that produce no tangible value beyond network security.

💡 The Critique

Critics argue: "What if all that computational power was directed toward something useful instead of solving meaningless puzzles?" Kenostod answers this question with PoRV.

🚀 What is Proof-of-Residual-Value?

🧠 The Kenostod Innovation

Proof-of-Residual-Value (PoRV) replaces meaningless hash puzzles with real enterprise computational tasks. Miners don't waste energy — they perform AI training, data analysis, scientific simulations, and other valuable computations.

Figure: PoRV mining — creating real value while securing the blockchain

How PoRV Works

  • Enterprise Submits Task: Companies pay to have computations performed
  • Task Distributed: Network breaks task into pieces for miners
  • Miners Compute: Real AI/ML work is performed instead of hash puzzles
  • Results Verified: Network validates computation accuracy
  • Rewards Distributed: Miners earn KENO + RVTs for verified work

💎 Residual Value Tokens (RVTs)

Here's where it gets exciting. When you mine via PoRV, you don't just earn KENO — you earn RVTs (Residual Value Tokens), which are royalty-bearing NFTs.

RVT Benefits

  • Passive Royalties: Earn income every time enterprises use your computational results commercially
  • Perpetual Income: RVTs continue generating royalties forever
  • Tradeable Assets: Sell your RVTs to other users
  • Tiered Rarity: Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers based on contribution value

✅ Real Example

You help train an AI model for a healthcare company. Every time that AI is used to analyze patient data, you receive a micro-royalty payment. This continues for years after your initial mining contribution.

📊 Economic Model

PoRV creates a deflationary tokenomics model:

  • 50% to RVT Holders: Royalties distributed to miners who created value
  • 40% Burned: KENO is permanently destroyed, reducing supply
  • 10% Treasury: Funds ongoing development and ecosystem growth

💡 Deflationary Pressure

Unlike Bitcoin where mining creates inflation, PoRV actually makes KENO more scarce over time. More enterprise usage = more KENO burned = higher value for remaining tokens.
