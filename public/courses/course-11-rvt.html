💎 Understanding RVTs

Residual Value Tokens (RVTs) are unique NFTs that represent your contribution to enterprise computations. Unlike regular NFTs, RVTs generate perpetual royalty income.

Figure: RVT portfolio dashboard showing holdings and royalty earnings

💡 Passive Income Machine

Every time an enterprise uses the AI model or computational result you helped create, you receive a micro-payment. This continues forever, creating a stream of passive income.

🏆 RVT Tier System

RVTs come in four tiers based on the value of your computational contribution:

🥉 Bronze

Standard contribution

1x Royalty Rate

🥈 Silver

Above-average

2x Royalty Rate

🥇 Gold

High-value work

5x Royalty Rate

💎 Platinum

Exceptional

10x Royalty Rate

📊 Portfolio Management

  • View Holdings: See all RVTs you own and their current tier
  • Track Earnings: Monitor daily, weekly, and monthly royalty income
  • Usage Analytics: See how often your computational results are used
  • Trade RVTs: Buy/sell RVTs on the marketplace

✅ Diversification Strategy

Like stocks, don't put all your eggs in one basket. Aim to collect RVTs from different industries (healthcare AI, financial modeling, climate research) to diversify your royalty streams.
