🏢 Course 12: Enterprise Task Submission PREMIUM

📖 4 Lessons ⏱️ ~35 minutes 🎯 Advanced Level 🏆 250 KENO upon completion

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Understand how enterprises submit computational tasks to the network
  • Learn the different types of enterprise tasks available
  • Navigate the task marketplace to find high-value opportunities
  • Submit and complete enterprise tasks to earn KENO and RVTs

🏢 Enterprise Tasks Explained

Enterprise tasks are real computational jobs submitted by companies who need distributed processing power. When you complete these tasks, you earn both KENO rewards and RVT royalty rights.

Figure: How enterprise tasks flow from submission to completion and payment

💡 Win-Win Economics

Enterprises get access to distributed computing at lower costs than cloud services. Miners earn more than traditional PoW mining. Everyone benefits!

📋 Task Types

  • AI/ML Training: Train machine learning models on distributed datasets
  • Data Processing: Clean, transform, and analyze large datasets
  • Scientific Simulations: Climate models, drug discovery, physics simulations
  • Rendering: 3D graphics, video processing, image generation
  • Financial Modeling: Risk analysis, market predictions, algorithmic trading

⚙️ Task Submission Workflow

  • Browse Marketplace: View available tasks filtered by type, reward, and complexity
  • Claim Task: Reserve a task (locked to you for completion)
  • Download Data: Receive encrypted task data package
  • Compute Locally: Your hardware performs the required computation
  • Submit Results: Upload completed work to the network
  • Verification: Network validates your results
  • Receive Rewards: KENO + RVT deposited to your wallet

✅ Quality Assurance

Results are verified by multiple nodes to ensure accuracy. High-quality work builds your reputation, unlocking access to premium high-paying tasks.

  • Complete and submit work
