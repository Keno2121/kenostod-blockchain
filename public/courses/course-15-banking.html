🏦 Course 15: Banking & Fiat Integration PREMIUM

📖 4 Lessons ⏱️ ~35 minutes 🎯 Advanced Level 🏆 250 KENO upon completion

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Understand how Kenostod bridges cryptocurrency and traditional banking
  • Learn the deposit and withdrawal process for USD
  • Navigate compliance requirements (KYC/AML)
  • Use PayPal integration for fiat on/off ramps

🌉 The Fiat Bridge

Kenostod connects the cryptocurrency world with traditional banking, allowing users to move money between KENO and USD seamlessly.

Figure: The bridge between traditional banking and KENO

💡 Why This Matters

Most cryptocurrency platforms make it difficult to convert to traditional currency. Kenostod's integrated banking makes it easy to use your KENO earnings in the real world.

💵 Deposits & Withdrawals

Deposit USD to Buy KENO

  • Connect PayPal: Link your PayPal account
  • Enter Amount: Choose how much USD to deposit
  • Confirm Exchange Rate: See current KENO/USD rate
  • Complete Transaction: Funds available in minutes

Withdraw KENO to USD

  • Initiate Withdrawal: Select KENO amount to sell
  • Verify Identity: Complete KYC if not already done
  • Receive USD: Funds sent to PayPal

📋 Compliance Requirements

  • KYC (Know Your Customer): Identity verification required for fiat transactions
  • AML (Anti-Money Laundering): Transaction monitoring for suspicious activity
  • Transaction Limits: Daily/monthly limits based on verification level

✅ Your Privacy

Crypto-to-crypto transactions remain private. KYC is only required when interfacing with traditional banking (fiat on/off ramps).
