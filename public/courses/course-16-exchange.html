📊 Order Book Basics

An order book is a list of all buy and sell orders for a trading pair, showing the prices people are willing to pay or accept.

Figure: Order book showing buy (bid) and sell (ask) orders

💡 Bid vs Ask

Bid: The highest price buyers are willing to pay.
Ask: The lowest price sellers are willing to accept.
The difference is called the "spread."

📋 Order Types

  • Market Order: Buy/sell immediately at current best price
  • Limit Order: Set your price and wait for the market to match it
  • Stop-Loss: Automatically sell if price drops below threshold
  • Take-Profit: Automatically sell when price reaches your target

✅ When to Use Which

Use market orders when speed matters more than price. Use limit orders when you want to control your entry/exit price.
