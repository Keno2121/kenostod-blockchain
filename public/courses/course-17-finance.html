📊 Budgeting Basics

A budget is a plan for your money. It helps you understand where your money goes and ensures you're directing it toward your goals.

Figure: The pillars of personal financial health

The 50/30/20 Rule

  • 50% Needs: Rent, utilities, food, transportation
  • 30% Wants: Entertainment, dining out, hobbies
  • 20% Savings: Emergency fund, investments, debt payoff

🛡️ Emergency Fund

An emergency fund protects you from unexpected expenses like medical bills, car repairs, or job loss.

💡 How Much to Save?

Aim for 3-6 months of living expenses in an easily accessible savings account. Start with $1,000 as your first milestone.

💳 Debt Management

  • List All Debts: Know exactly what you owe and interest rates
  • Minimum Payments: Always pay at least the minimum
  • Debt Avalanche: Pay extra on highest-interest debt first
  • Debt Snowball: Or pay smallest balance first for motivation
