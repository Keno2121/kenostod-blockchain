📅 Why Scheduled Payments Matter

Traditional cryptocurrencies require manual action for every payment. Want to pay rent monthly? You have to remember to send it manually each month. Kenostod changes this with protocol-level scheduled payments.

💡 No Smart Contracts Required

Unlike Ethereum where scheduling requires complex smart contracts and gas fees for each execution, Kenostod builds this functionality directly into the blockchain protocol. Simpler, cheaper, and more reliable.

📋 Types of Scheduled Payments

One-Time Future Payments

Schedule a single payment to execute at a specific future date and time.

  • Set the recipient, amount, and exact execution timestamp
  • Funds are locked but visible in your wallet until execution
  • Cancel anytime before the scheduled time

Recurring Payments

Automate regular payments with flexible schedules:

  • Daily: Perfect for micro-payments, daily allowances
  • Weekly: Great for payroll, subscription services
  • Monthly: Ideal for rent, loan payments, memberships

Figure: Visual representation of scheduled recurring payments

⚙️ How It Works

  • Create Schedule: Define recipient, amount, start date, and frequency
  • Sign Authorization: Digitally sign to authorize future executions
  • Balance Reserved: System ensures sufficient balance for next payment
  • Automatic Execution: Network processes payment at scheduled time
  • Notification: Both parties notified of each execution

✅ Safety Features

You can cancel any scheduled payment before it executes. For recurring payments, cancellation stops all future executions but doesn't affect already-completed payments.
