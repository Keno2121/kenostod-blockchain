KENO introduces a revolutionary asset class: the Knowledge Utility Token (KUT). Unlike traditional cryptocurrencies acquired through speculation, KENO is exclusively earned through education and enables arbitrage access through proprietary Flash Arbitrage Loan (FAL) technology.

Kenostod Academy is the educational platform that issues KENO tokens to students who complete its 21-course blockchain curriculum. Graduates enter a perpetual profit-sharing system, creating the first truly democratized wealth-building model in crypto.
