1. Abstract

This proposal requests the creation of a RVT-USDC perpetual contract on Hyperliquid, where the underlying index is the Compute Demand Index (CDI) — a real-time oracle measuring AI/ML compute demand across the Sovereign Economy's PoRV (Proof-of-Residual-Value) mining network.

RVT-USDC is not a token perpetual. It is a compute commodity perpetual — the first of its kind on any blockchain. Traders speculate on AI compute demand. Miners hedge royalty risk. The CDI provides the feed.

2. Background: PoRV & RVTs

Proof-of-Residual-Value (PoRV) is the Sovereign Economy's consensus layer that converts useful AI/ML compute work into economic value. The flow:

Enterprise submits AI/ML job
Any enterprise client submits a compute job (image recognition, LLM inference, data processing) to the PoRV network. They pay in USDC or KENO.
PoRV miner completes the job
A verified miner solves the job. Completion is recorded on the Kenostod chain with cryptographic proof.
Miner receives RVT + upfront KENO
The miner earns: (a) upfront KENO payment, and (b) a Residual Value Token (RVT) entitling them to a perpetual royalty on all future commercial use of the job output.
RVT is tradeable on HL as RVT-USDC perp
The miner can hedge or monetize their royalty exposure by trading the RVT-USDC perpetual. Long = bet on more AI demand. Short = hedge against compute slowdown.
Enterprise use → royalty distribution
Every time an enterprise commercializes an AI output, royalties flow automatically to RVT holders — forever. The RVT perp prices this future cashflow stream.

3. RVT Tiers

RVTs are issued in four tiers based on job complexity and miner performance:

🥉 Bronze — 5% royalty
🥈 Silver — 10% royalty

Higher-tier RVTs carry higher CDI weight — a Diamond RVT contributes 4× the CDI signal of a Bronze.
