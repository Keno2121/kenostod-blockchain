KENO — Solana Bridge Guide

Step-by-step: Wrap KENO on Solana via Wormhole Portal. Tap any value to copy.

🌉 Wormhole Portal is the most battle-tested BSC → Solana bridge. This creates Portal-wrapped KENO (an SPL token) that can be traded on Raydium and Meteora — Solana's top DEXes.
★
What You Need Before Starting
  • ◆ MetaMask connected to BSC Mainnet with your safe wallet (0x4AA7…849)
  • ◆ Phantom or Solflare wallet — install from phantom.app or solflare.com. Create a new Solana wallet and save the seed phrase.
  • ◆ ~0.01 SOL in your Solana wallet for account creation (rent fee). Buy SOL on any exchange or use a CEX withdrawal.
  • ◆ ~0.005 BNB in your BSC wallet for gas on the bridge transaction.
  • ◆ KENO tokens to bridge — only bridge what you need for liquidity seeding. Do NOT bridge your full treasury.

4
Approve & Send the Bridge Transaction
  • 1 Click "Next" — Portal will show a summary of the transfer
  • 2 Click "Approve" — MetaMask pops up to approve KENO spend (one-time)
  • 3 Click "Transfer" — MetaMask pops up for the actual bridge tx (~0.003 BNB gas)
  • 4 Wait 15–30 minutes for Wormhole guardians to sign the VAA (cross-chain proof)
  • 5 Click "Redeem" on the Solana side — Phantom pops up (~0.01 SOL to create token account)
  • 6 Done — wrapped KENO (wKENO SPL) appears in your Phantom wallet
⚠️ Save the Wormhole transaction link after Step 3. If the page closes, go to portalbridge.com → "Redeem" tab and paste your BSC tx hash to resume.
5
Record Your Wrapped KENO (SPL) Address

After redeeming, Wormhole creates a unique SPL token address for wrapped KENO on Solana. You need this for everything below.

  • 1 Open Phantom wallet → tap the token to expand it
  • 2 Tap the contract address shown under the token name
  • 3 Copy it and save it — this is your Solana wKENO mint address
  • 4 Verify it on solscan.io — search the mint address to confirm your balance
