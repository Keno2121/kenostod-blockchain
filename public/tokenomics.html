Token Utility

What KENO Does

KENO is not speculative. Every unit of utility is tied to a real economic action inside the Sovereign Economy.

Education Mining (PoRV — Proof of Real Value)
Students earn KENO by completing Academy courses. 21 courses (a Fibonacci number). Each completion triggers an on-chain reward event. KENO is the only educational token earned — not purchased — to access learning.
KUTL Card — Spend Layer
KENO earned loads directly onto the KUTL Card via the UTL Protocol. Cardholders spend KENO at point-of-sale. Card cashback is paid back in KENO. Under the Wyoming SPDI charter, this card operates under a full banking charter — not a money transmitter license.
UTLFarm — Staking & Yield
KENO holders stake to UTLFarm. Yield is calculated using Euler's continuous compounding formula (A = Pe^rt) — meaning stakers always receive the mathematical maximum. No other staking protocol applies continuous compounding by default.
Governance — Sovereign Council
100,000 KENO + 15 completed courses grants Sovereign Council status. 10,000 KENO + 5 courses = District Governor. Governance weight is knowledge-gated, not wealth-gated.
Guardian Node Access
1,000 KENO staked + Solar Bunker hardware = Guardian Node status. Nodes validate transactions, earn rewards, and form the mesh network infrastructure for Phase 3 sovereign chain.
Phase 3 — Native Gas Token
When the Sovereign Economy Network launches on Cosmos SDK, KENO becomes the native gas token. The Wyoming SPDI operates as an institutional node validator. BSC KENO bridges natively via IBC.
