## Step 2: Register Online

**Information You'll Need:**
- LLC Name: **Kenostod Blockchain Academy LLC**
- Registered Agent: Your address OR a registered agent service
- Member(s): Your name (Nickeo Coleman)
- Business Address: Your address
- Business Purpose: "Blockchain education and technology services"

**Process:**
1. Go to state website
2. Search if name is available
3. Fill out Articles of Organization form
4. Pay filing fee
5. Download/save your Certificate of Formation

## Step 3: Get EIN (Same Day - Free)

**Website:** https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online

**Information Needed:**
- LLC Name: Kenostod Blockchain Academy LLC
- Your SSN
- Business Address
- Type: LLC
- Reason: Started new business

**Result:** EIN Confirmation Letter (save PDF!)
